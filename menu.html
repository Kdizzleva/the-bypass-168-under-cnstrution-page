Our Menu

Fresh, house-made favorites and a few Bypass originals. Ask about daily features and sides.

Appetizers

  • Steamed Shrimp

    Lightly seasoned with old bay and lemon; served with drawn butter, cocktail sauce, and fresh lemon.

  • Sesame Tuna Slices*

    Fresh tuna encrusted in sesame seeds and grilled to a perfect medium rare; served with house sauce.

  • Nachos

    Warm tortilla chips layered with melted cheddar and pepper jack, lettuce, sour cream, and pico de gallo.

  • Fried Veggie Basket

    Mushrooms, dill pickle spears, and jalapeños in our secret batter; served with ranch and horseradish sauce.

  • Crab Dip

    Rich and crabby, served warm with pita wedges.

  • Wings

    Crispy wings with your choice of sauces: BBQ, Mild, Hot, Old Bay, or Garlic Parmesan.

  • Mozzarella Rounds

    House-breaded mozzarella, fried and served with marinara.

  • The Triple Bypass

    Pick any three house dips (pizza dip, buffalo chicken dip, spinach artichoke dip) with tortilla chips and pita wedges.

  • Homemade Hummus Platter

    Served with celery, carrot sticks, olives, feta, and pita wedges.

  • Chips & Salsa

    Warm tortilla chips with house salsa — chunky or smooth.

Sandwiches & Wraps

Served with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickle, and your choice of side.

  • Egg Salad Sandwich

    “Rock star quality!” — Tara’s legendary no-pickle recipe.

  • Veggie Wrap

    House hummus, fresh spinach, tomato, pickled onions, and feta in a flour tortilla.

  • Sriracha Tuna Wrap*

    Yellowfin tuna tossed in sriracha, wrapped with lettuce, onions, and sriracha mayo.

  • Chicken Sandwich

    Lightly seasoned grilled or fried chicken breast on a kaiser roll.

  • Crab Cake Sandwich

    Our crabiest crab cake on a kaiser roll with lettuce, tomato, and tartar sauce.

  • BBQ Sandwich

    Carolina pulled pork on a kaiser roll with a side of coleslaw.

  • BLT

    A classic — bacon, lettuce, and tomato on your choice of bread.

  • Burger*

    Half-pound burger on a kaiser roll. Build it your way with our add-ons.

Sandwich & Burger Add-Ons: House sauces, American, feta, blue cheese, sautéed or pickled onions, sautéed mushrooms, coleslaw, bacon, and sautéed crab.

Entrées

Served with a small House or Caesar salad and your choice of one side.

  • Tuna Tacos*

    Tuna blackened or grilled to medium-rare with cabbage, pico, shredded cheese, chipotle-lime sauce, and a drizzle of tropical hot sauce.

  • Ribs

    Slow-cooked in our coffee BBQ sauce. Half or full rack.

  • Chicken Piccata

    Chicken breast in lemon, butter, white wine, and caper sauce.

  • Pork BBQ Platter

    Generous helping of grass-fed Carolina pork BBQ with coleslaw.

  • Chipotle-Lime Fried Shrimp Dinner

    Large shrimp in chipotle-lime batter, fried crisp; served with cocktail sauce.

  • Chicken Florentine

    Grilled chicken breast over spinach artichoke dip, garnished with marinated tomatoes.

  • NY Strip*

    Hand-cut New York strip cooked to your liking; finished with sun-dried tomato or blue-cheese compound butter.

  • Crab Cakes

    Two house crab cakes, pan-seared to perfection.

Soup & Salads

  • House Salad

    Romaine with cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, onions, cheese, croutons, and bacon bits. Dressings: House Lemon Basil, Ranch, Blue Cheese, Honey Mustard, Light Italian, Thousand Island.

  • Caesar Salad

    Crisp romaine tossed with Caesar dressing, shredded Parmesan, and croutons.

  • Greek Salad

    Romaine with house lemon-basil dressing, tomatoes, onions, olives, cucumbers, croutons, and feta.

  • Soup of the Day

    Ask your server for today’s selection.

Add-ons available: chicken, tuna, or shrimp.

Sides

Always available: French fries, baked potato, coleslaw. Ask about our daily fresh sides.
