Menu

All menus and pricing are subject to change. Ask your server for daily features and limited-time items.

Appetizers

  • Off-Ramp Nachos

    Fresh tortilla chips loaded with our house “man dip,” covered in melted cheddar and topped with lettuce, sour cream, pico de gallo, and jalapeños.

  • Construction Cone Mozzarella Rounds

    Hand-breaded fresh mozzarella balls, fried golden and served with marinara.

  • Eastbound Steamed Shrimp

    Steamed shrimp, lightly seasoned with Old Bay. Served with drawn butter and cocktail sauce. Available in half-pound or full-pound.

  • Mile-Marker Sesame Tuna*

    Fresh tuna encrusted in sesame seeds and grilled to medium-rare. Served sliced with our house dipping sauce.

  • Roadside Wings

    Perfectly cooked wings (6 or 12) with your choice of sauce or dry rub. Served with celery and blue cheese or ranch.

Brunch at the Bypass

Available during posted brunch hours. Ask your server for today’s specials.

  • Morning Rush Platter

    Two eggs your way, choice of bacon or sausage, breakfast potatoes, and toast or biscuit.

  • Detour Benedict*

    Toasted English muffin, ham or bacon, poached eggs, and hollandaise, served with breakfast potatoes.

  • Overpass Omelet

    Three-egg omelet with choice of cheese and fillings. Served with breakfast potatoes and toast.

  • Slow Lane French Toast

    Thick-cut French toast with powdered sugar and syrup, plus choice of bacon or sausage.

Bypass Burgers*

All burgers are made with fresh beef and served with fries. Substitute a side salad or another side for an upcharge.

  • The Bypass Burger*

    Classic build with American cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and house burger sauce on a toasted bun.

  • Gridlock Double Stack*

    Two patties with melted cheese, grilled onions, pickles, and sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

  • Fast Lane Bacon Cheddar*

    Bacon, cheddar, lettuce, tomato, and smoky mayo.

  • Exit 168 Mushroom Swiss*

    Sautéed mushrooms, Swiss cheese, and garlic mayo.

  • Shoulder Season Veggie Burger

    Veggie patty with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and sauce. (Can be prepared gluten-friendly on a lettuce wrap.)

Handhelds

Served with fries unless otherwise noted.

  • Rest-Stop Chicken Sandwich

    Grilled or fried chicken breast with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and mayo on a toasted bun.

  • Road-Crew Club

    Triple-stacked turkey, ham, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayo on toasted bread.

  • Shoulder-Shredder Pork BBQ

    Carolina-style pulled pork on a toasted bun with coleslaw on the side.

  • Turnpike Philly

    Sliced steak or chicken with peppers, onions, and melted cheese on a hoagie roll.

Chicken & Platters

Platters are served with your choice of two sides unless otherwise noted.

  • Chicken Piccata

    Chicken breast sautéed in lemon, butter, white wine, and caper sauce.

  • Chicken Florentine

    Grilled chicken breast over creamy spinach-artichoke dip, garnished with marinated tomatoes.

  • Chipotle-Lime Fried Shrimp Dinner

    Large shrimp in chipotle-lime batter, fried crisp and served with cocktail sauce.

  • Pork BBQ Platter

    Generous helping of Carolina pork BBQ, served with coleslaw.

  • Slow-Lane Ribs

    Slow-cooked ribs in our coffee BBQ sauce. Available as half or full rack.

  • NY Strip*

    Hand-cut New York strip grilled to your liking, finished with sun-dried tomato or blue-cheese compound butter.

  • Crab Cakes

    Two house crab cakes, pan-seared to perfection.

Tacos & Tuna*

  • Tuna Tacos*

    Tuna blackened or grilled to medium-rare with cabbage, pico, shredded cheese, chipotle-lime sauce, and a drizzle of tropical hot sauce.

  • Roadside Shrimp Tacos

    Chipotle-lime shrimp with cabbage, pico, shredded cheese, and chipotle-lime sauce.

  • Fish-in-the-Fast-Lane Tacos*

    Fresh fish (blackened or grilled) with cabbage, pico, cheese, and sauce. Ask about today’s catch.

  • Seared Ahi Plate*

    Sesame-crusted tuna, seared to medium-rare and served with rice and vegetables.

Soup & Salads

  • House Salad

    Romaine with cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, onions, cheese, croutons, and bacon bits. Dressings: House Lemon Basil, Ranch, Blue Cheese, Honey Mustard, Light Italian, Thousand Island.

  • Caesar Salad

    Crisp romaine tossed with Caesar dressing, shredded Parmesan, and croutons.

  • Greek Salad

    Romaine with house lemon-basil dressing, tomatoes, onions, olives, cucumbers, croutons, and feta.

  • Soup of the Day

    Ask your server for today’s selection.

Add-ons available: chicken, tuna, or shrimp.

Kids Menu

For younger guests. Includes one kids side and a drink.

  • Kids Burger

    Plain or with cheese, served on a bun.

  • Kids Chicken Tenders

    Crispy chicken tenders with choice of dipping sauce.

  • Kids Grilled Cheese

    Melted cheese on toasted bread.

  • Kids Mac & Cheese

    Classic creamy mac and cheese.

Sides

French fries, baked potato, coleslaw, seasonal vegetables, side salad, and other daily fresh sides. Ask your server for today’s options.

*Consuming raw or undercooked meats, seafood, or eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illness. Some items may be served undercooked upon request.
